## Ezekiel 39: The Defeat of Gog and the Restoration

Abrahamic and Davidic covenants are the foundation of God's promises to Israel.

First two chapters covered the promise of a king
Next two chapters covered the promise of land and prosperity.
Next two chapters covered the promise of people of peace
Now move into final section, a God dwelling among His people.
- single chapter shows how God makes Himself known to the Gentile nations of earth
- second part is nine chapters by itself explaining how God will dwell with His people.
