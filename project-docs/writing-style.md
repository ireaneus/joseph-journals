# Writing Style Guide

## Tone

- Pastoral, clear, and warm
- Doctrinally grounded
- Not overly academic

## Structure

- Strong introduction (hook + conviction)
- Clear central idea
- Logical flow
- Strong application

## Scripture

- Use full quotations when appropriate
- Do not take verses out of context
- Maintain accuracy

## Avoid

- Redundancy
- Vague statements
- Overly complex wording

## Goal

Clarity + conviction + faithfulness to the text
