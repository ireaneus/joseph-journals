*For Eliana — "My God has answered"*  
*rooted in the love He grew,*  
*planted by the God who knew*  
*every green thing — and knew you.*  

*Genesis 1:9–13 · ESV*

---

## Page 1 — Let the Waters Gather
🌊

*Gather,* He said,  
*waters, come —*  
find your place  
and be as one,  
roll together,  
deep and wide,  
let the dry land  
step outside.

> *"And God said, 'Let the waters under the heavens be gathered together into one place, and let the dry land appear.'"*  
> **Genesis 1:9a (ESV)**

> *God called the waters and they came — He only had to speak their name. He gathers what is scattered, little one, and He will always gather you.*

---

## Page 2 — And It Was So
🏝️

The waters rushed  
to where He said,  
the land rose up  
and lifted its head —  
dry and wide  
and ready to hold  
every story  
yet to be told.

> *"And it was so."*  
> **Genesis 1:9b (ESV)**

> *God spoke, and the whole earth obeyed. The seas went where He sent them. The land rose where He willed it. What God makes room for, He fills with good things — and that room includes you.*

---

## Page 3 — He Named What He Made
🌍

He called the dry ground  
*Earth* — just so.  
He called the gathered  
waters *Seas* below.  
He named them both  
with care and love,  
then looked upon them  
from above.

> *"God called the dry land Earth, and the waters that were gathered together he called Seas. And God saw that it was good."*  
> **Genesis 1:10 (ESV)**

> *God names what He loves. He named the earth. He named the seas. And, Eliana, He has named you — and looked at you and called you good.*

---

## Page 4 — Let the Earth Bring Forth
🌱

*Grow,* He whispered  
to the ground —  
and green things stirred  
without a sound,  
pushing up through  
dust and dark,  
each one carrying  
its own seed-spark.

> *"And God said, 'Let the earth sprout vegetation, plants yielding seed, and fruit trees bearing fruit in which is their seed, each according to its kind, on the earth.' And it was so."*  
> **Genesis 1:11 (ESV)**

> *God said grow — and the whole earth grew. Every leaf, every flower, every seed carries the fingerprint of the God who made it. You were made by that same tender hand.*

---

## Page 5 — Each After Its Kind
🌸

The grasses rose  
in green and gold,  
the fruit trees  
held their seeds to hold —  
each one knew  
just what to be,  
each one made  
exactly free.

> *"The earth brought forth vegetation, plants yielding seed according to their own kinds, and trees bearing fruit in which is their seed, each according to its kind. And God saw that it was good."*  
> **Genesis 1:12 (ESV)**

> *Everything God made, He made on purpose — each seed knowing what it holds, each tree knowing what it grows. You too were made on purpose, little one, exactly as He meant.*

---

## Page 6 — The Third Day
🌅

The evening fell  
on root and vine,  
the morning came  
with gold sunshine —  
the third day done,  
and earth was green,  
the fullest day  
the world had seen.

> *"And there was evening and there was morning, the third day."*  
> **Genesis 1:13 (ESV)**

> *Three days done — light, sky, and living earth. God is building something beautiful, one day at a time. He is building something beautiful in you to enjoy.*

---

## ✦ Closing

> *"For as the earth brings forth its sprouts,*  
> *and as a garden causes what is sown in it to sprout up,*  
> *so the Lord God will cause righteousness and praise*  
> *to sprout up before all the nations."*  
> **Isaiah 61:11 · ESV**
