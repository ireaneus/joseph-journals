*For Eliana — "My God has answered"*  
*held beneath the stars He hung,*  
*loved before their light was sung,*  
*known before the skies were young.*  

*Genesis 1:14–19 · ESV*

---

## Page 1 — Lights in the Expanse
🌟

He looked at the sky —  
so wide, so bare —  
and called for lights  
to fill the air:  
*signs and seasons,*  
*days and years,*  
*a clock of glory  
for all who have ears.*  

> *"And God said, 'Let there be lights in the expanse of the heavens to separate the day from the night. And let them be for signs and for seasons, and for days and years.'"*  
> **Genesis 1:14 (ESV)**

> *Before the first sunrise, God planned it — light to mark the days, to order the seasons, to tell the story of time. Every morning that breaks is His faithful keeping of that promise.*

---

## Page 2 — To Give Light on the Earth
☀️

Not just for beauty,  
not just for show —  
God hung the lights  
so the earth below  
would never be left  
without a glow,  
without a guide  
wherever you go.

> *"And let them be lights in the expanse of the heavens to give light upon the earth."*  
> **Genesis 1:15a (ESV)**

> *God made the lights to shine downward — toward the earth, toward us. He is always giving light, always shining toward what He loves. His face is turned toward you.*

---

## Page 3 — And It Was So
✅

He spoke —  
and the sky filled up,  
light poured in  
like an upturned cup,  
and all the heavens  
held their breath  
at the glory burning  
overhead.

> *"And it was so."*  
> **Genesis 1:15b (ESV)**

> *Three words — and the sky blazed with light. God does not struggle to keep His promises. He speaks, and it is so. What He has spoken over you will surely come to pass.*

---

## Page 4 — The Greater and the Lesser
🌕

He made the great light  
for the day,  
He made the lesser  
for the night's way —  
and with them, stars  
too many to see,  
a scattered glory  
wild and free.

> *"And God made the two great lights — the greater light to rule the day and the lesser light to rule the night — and the stars."*  
> **Genesis 1:16 (ESV)**

> *Sun for the day, moon for the night, and then, as if for joy, He scattered the stars — each one placed with perfect care, filling every corner of the dark. There is no night so deep His light cannot find you, no shadow beyond the reach of His love.*

---

## Page 5 — He Set Them in Place
📍

He didn't just make them —  
He placed them right,  
each one set  
at its perfect height,  
to rule their hours   
with faithful care,  
a God who orders  
what He puts there.

> *"And God set them in the expanse of the heavens to give light on the earth, to rule over the day and over the night, and to separate the light from the darkness. And God saw that it was good."*  
> **Genesis 1:17–18 (ESV)**

> *God does not make things and leave them to wander — He places them, orders them, and holds them in their purpose. He has placed you here, in this family, in this moment, with that same care.*

---

## Page 6 — The Fourth Day
🌅

The evening came  
and stars appeared,  
the morning broke  
and light was cleared —  
the fourth day done,  
the sky full bright,  
a faithful God  
who rules the light.

> *"And there was evening and there was morning, the fourth day."*  
> **Genesis 1:19 (ESV)**

> *Four days done — and still God is not hurrying. He takes His time, and every day is full of purpose. His patience is not slowness; it is love working carefully.*

---

## ✦ Closing

> *"He determines the number of the stars;*  
> *he gives to all of them their names."*  
> **Psalm 147:4 · ESV**
