# In the Beginning
### A Nursery Book of Creation
*Genesis 1:1–3 · ESV*

---

*For Eliana — "My God has answered"*  
*held in the hands of the God who made the light,*  
*loved before the darkness gave way to bright,*  
*known before the morning, little one so dear.*

---

## Page 1 — Before It All
🌌

Before the world,  
before the sky,  
before a single breath or sigh,  
God was always there on high.

> *Before a star could shine or sing, before the dawn of anything — God always was, and always is, and, little one, you're already His.*

---

## Page 2 — God Made Everything
🌍

God made the heavens,  
God made the earth,  
by His word alone  
He gave all things birth.

> *"In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth."*  
> **Genesis 1:1 (ESV)**  
>
> *He only had to speak the word — and mountains rose and rivers stirred. That powerful, that good, that near — that is the God who holds you, dear.*


---

## Page 3 — Dark and Still
🌑

The earth was dark,  
and still, and deep,  
like a quiet world  
still fast asleep.

> *"The earth was without form and void, and darkness was over the face of the deep."*  
> **Genesis 1:2a (ESV)**  
>
> *But empty dark is not the end — God had a plan around each bend. He holds the dark and holds the light, and holds you safely through the night.*

---

## Page 4 — The Spirit Near
🕊️

God's Spirit moved  
so soft, so near,  
over the waters,  
*"I am here, I am here."*

> *"And the Spirit of God was hovering over the face of the waters."*  
> **Genesis 1:2b (ESV)**  
>
> *Like a father bends to kiss his child, God hovered near the dark and wild — so tender, so gentle, so close, so true,
that same sweet Spirit hovers over you.*

---

## Page 5 — Let There Be Light!
☀️

Then God said softly,  
then God said bright—  
*"Let there be*  
*light, light, LIGHT!"*  

> *"And God said, 'Let there be light,' and there was light."*  
> **Genesis 1:3a (ESV)**  
>
> *One word from God and darkness fled — light burst forth and glory spread. His word is good, His word is true, and, Eliana, His word made you.*

---

## ✦ Closing

> *"For God, who said, 'Let light shine out of darkness,' has shone in our hearts to give the light of the knowledge of the glory of God in the face of Jesus Christ."*  
> **2 Corinthians 4:6 · ESV**
