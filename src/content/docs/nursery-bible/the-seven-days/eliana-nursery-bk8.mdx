*For Eliana — "My God has answered"*  
*loved by the One who made the wild,*  
*the God who knows each creature mild,*  
*who made them all — and made you, child.*  

*Genesis 1:24–25 · ESV*

---

## Page 1 — Let the Earth Bring Forth
🐘

*Bring forth,* He said  
to the waiting ground —  
and creatures rose  
without a sound,  
padding, lumbering,  
creeping, small,  
the earth obeying  
God's great call.

> *"And God said, 'Let the earth bring forth living creatures according to their kinds — livestock and creeping things and beasts of the earth according to their kinds.'"*  
> **Genesis 1:24a (ESV)**

> *God called to the earth and the earth answered — life came pouring out of the ground at His word. He is a God who calls things forth, who speaks and living things appear.*

---

## Page 2 — And It Was So
✅

The lion stretched,  
the lamb stood still,  
the beetle crept  
beneath the hill —  
each one exactly  
as He planned,  
each one held  
in His good hand.

> *"And it was so."*  
> **Genesis 1:24b (ESV)**

> *Four words again — and it was so. God never has to say a thing twice. What He speaks comes to life, comes to pass, comes exactly as He intended.*

---

## Page 3 — Beasts and Cattle and Creeping Things
🦁

The great beasts roamed  
the open land,  
the cattle grazed  
as God had planned,  
the small things crept  
through root and stone —  
not one of them  
was left alone.

> *"And God made the beasts of the earth according to their kinds and the livestock according to their kinds, and everything that creeps on the ground according to its kind."*  
> **Genesis 1:25a (ESV)**

> *Every kind of creature — great and small, wild and gentle, hidden and bold — God made them all. Not one was forgotten, not one was an afterthought. He makes nothing by accident.*

---

## Page 4 — God Saw That It Was Good
✨

He looked at the lion,  
He looked at the lamb,  
He looked at the beetle  
and said, *I am  
the God who made you,  
the God who sees —  
and all of this  
is good to Me.*

> *"And God saw that it was good."*  
> **Genesis 1:25b (ESV)**

> *God looked at every creature — the fierce and the tiny, the hidden and the grand — and called it good. His eye misses nothing. His approval rests on everything He has made with care.*

---

## Page 5 — The Sixth Day Begins
🌅

The morning broke  
on paw and hoof,  
on wing and burrow,  
den and roof —  
the sixth day open,  
wide and new,  
and something greater  
still to do.

> *The earth was full — creature upon creature, kind upon kind, each one made and held and called good by the God who spoke them into being. And still, the sixth day was not finished. The best was yet to come.*

> *God was not done. He never rushes, and He never runs out. What He began on the sixth day, He would bring to its fullness — and all creation was waiting.*

---

## ✦ Closing

> *"O LORD, how manifold are your works!*  
> *In wisdom have you made them all;*  
> *the earth is full of your creatures."*  
> **Psalm 104:24 · ESV**
