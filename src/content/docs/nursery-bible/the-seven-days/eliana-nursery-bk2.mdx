# Before the Morning
### A Nursery Book of Chosen Love
*Psalm 78:3–7 · ESV*

---

*For Eliana — "My God has answered"*  
*chosen before the stars were hung,*  
*loved before the world was young,*  
*held before the morning sun.*

> *The moment you were born, His Word fell open*  
> *Psalm 78:5–6 — and God said, "She is the one."*

---

## Page 1 — A Story Worth Telling
📖

There is a story,  
old and deep,  
our fathers told it  
in our sleep —  
the glorious deeds  
of God to keep.

> *"things that we have heard and known,*
> *that our fathers have told us."*
> **Psalm 78:3 (ESV)**

> *Before your first breath broke the air,*
> *this story was already there —*
> *a song begun before your name,*
> *and, little one, you're why it came.*

---

## Page 2 — We Will Tell You
🕯️

We will not hide it,  
we will not sleep —  
these glorious deeds
are yours to keep.  
We'll tell you of  
the God who's deep.

> *"We will not hide them from their children,*
> *but tell to the coming generation*
> *the glorious deeds of the Lord,*
> *and his might, and the wonders that he has done."*
> **Psalm 78:4 (ESV)**

> *His might made mountains, split the sea,*
> *and, Eliana, His might made thee —*
> *every wonder He has done*
> *whispers, "She is my beloved one."*

---

## Page 3 — He Commanded It
⚖️

He didn't leave it  
up to chance —  
He set His word  
in advance,  
to pass His glory  
down the dance.

> *"He established a testimony in Jacob*
> *and appointed a law in Israel,*
> *which he commanded our fathers*
> *to teach to their children."*
> **Psalm 78:5 (ESV)**

> *This was no accident, no guess —*
> *God planned this, Eliana, nonetheless.*
> *He set His word before the years,*
> *and called your name before your tears.*

---

## Page 4 — You Were the Child
🌱

The children yet unborn —  
that's you,  
the one the Psalm  
was pointing to,  
the one God always  
had in view.

> *"that the next generation might know them,*
> *the children yet unborn,*
> *and arise and tell them to their children."*
> **Psalm 78:6 (ESV)**

> *Before the ink had dried on time,*
> *before the stars were set in rhyme,*
> *God wrote your name in His own hand —*
> *Eliana, chosen, planned.*

---

## Page 5 — Set Your Hope
⚓

So set your hope  
on God alone,  
for He has loved you  
as His own —  
His works will never  
leave you alone.

> *"so that they should set their hope in God*
> *and not forget the works of God,*
> *but keep his commandments."*
> **Psalm 78:7 (ESV)**

> *When questions come and shadows creep,*
> *His works are promises to keep —*
> *set your hope on Him alone,*
> *the God who chose you calls you His own.*

---

## ✦ Closing

*"...even as he chose us in him*  
*before the foundation of the world,*  
*that we should be holy and blameless before him."*  
**Ephesians 1:4 · ESV**
