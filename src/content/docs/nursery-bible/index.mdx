*Dear Eliana —*

Your name means *"My God has answered."*  
Before you could ask, He already had.

*These books were written for you — so that before you could read, someone would read to you the story that was always yours. The story of how God made the world, and why, and for who.*

One day you will read them yourself.  
And one day after that,  
perhaps you will read them  
to someone small who sits in your lap  
the way you once sat in ours.

*These words were written with love, and they point to a Love far greater.*

*— For Eliana, from Grandpa Haines*
