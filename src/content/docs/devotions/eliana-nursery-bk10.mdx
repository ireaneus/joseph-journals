*For Eliana — "My God has answered"*  
*held in the rest that Jesus won,*  
*loved by the Father through the Son,*  
*kept in the peace of the work that's done.*  

*Genesis 2:1–3 · ESV*

---

## Page 1 — It Was Finished
🌟

Six days of speaking,  
six days of making —  
light and sky  
and earth awaking,  
creature and creature,  
man and woman —  
and all of it  
was whole and good.

> *"Thus the heavens and the earth were finished, and all the host of them."*  
> **Genesis 2:1 (ESV)**

> *Finished — not abandoned, not set aside, but completed. God does not leave things half made. What He begins, He brings to fullness. The whole creation stood complete in His hands.*

---

## Page 2 — He Rested
🌙

On the seventh day  
God set it down —  
not from weariness,  
not with a frown,  
but because the work  
was wholly done,  
every creature made,  
every river run.

> *"And on the seventh day God finished his work that he had done, and he rested on the seventh day from all his work that he had done."*  
> **Genesis 2:2 (ESV)**

> *God did not rest because He was tired — He rested because He was finished. There is a difference. Tired rest says I cannot go on. Finished rest says it is complete. Nothing is missing. Nothing is left undone.*

---

## Page 3 — He Blessed the Day
✨

He blessed the seventh,  
set it apart —  
a holy day  
held near His heart,  
different from all  
the days before,  
a day that opened  
a different door.

> *"So God blessed the seventh day and made it holy, because on it God rested from all his work that he had done in creation."*  
> **Genesis 2:3 (ESV)**

> *God blessed a day — the only day He blessed in all of creation. He made it holy, set apart, different. This day was not just a pause in the week. It was a door God opened and said — come in, and rest with Me.*

---

## Page 4 — A Rest That Remains
🕊️

But the door stayed open —  
the rest was not closed,  
it waited through ages  
for all who would come,  
a rest still unfinished,  
a rest still undone,  
a rest that was pointing  
toward Someone to come.

> *"So then, there remains a Sabbath rest for the people of God, for whoever has entered God's rest has also rested from his works as God did from his."*  
> **Hebrews 4:9–10 (ESV)**

> *The rest of Day 7 was never just one day in one week. It was a promise written into creation — that one day God's people would enter a rest so complete, so finished, that they would never strive again. That rest has a name.*

---

## Page 5 — Come to Me
🙌

*Come,* He said,  
*all who are worn,*  
*all who are weary,*  
*all who are torn —*  
*I will give you  
rest so deep  
it is more than  
sleep, sleep, sleep.*

> *"Come to me, all who labor and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest."*  
> **Matthew 11:28 (ESV)**

> *Jesus stood up and said — I am the rest that creation has been waiting for. The seventh day was always pointing here, to Him, to the finished work of the cross. To believe in Him is to enter the rest God blessed at the beginning.*

---

## Page 6 — It Is Finished
✝️

Six days of making,  
one day of rest —  
and then in the fullness  
of time, God's best:  
a Son on a cross  
who bowed His head  
and said *it is finished* —  
and rose from the dead.

> *The seventh day whispered what the cross would one day say out loud — it is finished. The work is done. The rest is real. And all who come to Jesus find the rest that God blessed at the very beginning of the world.*

> *This is the rest He made you for, little one — not just sleep at the end of a long day, but peace with God that nothing can take away.*

---

## ✦ Closing

> *"So then, there remains a Sabbath rest for the people of God,*  
> *for whoever has entered God's rest*  
> *has also rested from his works as God did from his.*  
> *Let us therefore strive to enter that rest."*  
> **Hebrews 4:9–11 · ESV**
