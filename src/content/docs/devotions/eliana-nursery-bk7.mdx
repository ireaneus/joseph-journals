*For Eliana — "My God has answered"*  
*loved by the One who fills the sea,*  
*known by the God who set birds free,*  
*held in His hand so tenderly.*  

*Genesis 1:20–23 · ESV*

---

## Page 1 — Let the Waters Swarm
🐟

*Swarm,* He said  
to the deep below,  
*let living things  
begin to go —*  
darting, swimming,  
gliding through,  
filling the waters  
through and through.

> *"And God said, 'Let the waters swarm with swarms of living creatures.'"*  
> **Genesis 1:20a (ESV)**

> *God did not make a silent sea — He filled it with life, motion, and color. Every creature that stirs in the deep does so because God said swarm. He is a God who fills what He makes.*

---

## Page 2 — Let Birds Fly
🕊️

*Fly,* He said  
to the open sky,  
*let wings be made  
and let them try —*  
over the earth,  
across the face,  
filling the heavens  
with winged grace.

> *"And let birds fly above the earth across the expanse of the heavens."*  
> **Genesis 1:20b (ESV)**

> *God looked at the wide open sky and called for something to fill it — wings, and song, and motion. He made the sky and then filled it with joy. He does the same with every life He makes.*

---

## Page 3 — God Created
✨

Not shaped by chance,  
nor born of night —  
God spoke them out  
in the light, 
created every  
creature there,  
the sea below,  
the birds in air.

> *"So God created the great sea creatures and every living creature that moves, with which the waters swarm, according to their kinds, and every winged bird according to its kind. And God saw that it was good."*  
> **Genesis 1:21 (ESV)**

> *The word here is created — the same word as the very beginning. God made something genuinely new, full of life, each kind its own. And He looked at all of it and called it good.*

---

## Page 4 — God Blessed Them
🙌

He didn't just make them  
and walk away —  
He bent down low  
and began to say:  
*be fruitful, multiply,  
fill the sea,  
let the birds  
multiply and be free.*

> *"And God blessed them, saying, 'Be fruitful and multiply and fill the waters in the seas, and let birds multiply on the earth.'"*  
> **Genesis 1:22 (ESV)**

> *The first words God ever speaks to His creatures are a blessing. He opens His mouth and goodness comes out. This is who He is — and He speaks the same way to you.*

---

## Page 5 — The Fifth Day
🌅

The evening fell  
on fin and wing,  
the morning came  
and birds would sing —  
the fifth day done,  
the world alive,  
with every creature  
made to thrive.

> *"And there was evening and there was morning, the fifth day."*  
> **Genesis 1:23 (ESV)**

> *Five days in, and the world is alive with motion — swimming, soaring, singing. God makes things that move and breathe and fill the earth. He has never made anything He did not intend to flourish.*

---

## ✦ Closing

> *"Look at the birds of the air: they neither sow nor reap nor gather into barns, and yet your heavenly Father feeds them. Are you not of more value than they?"*  
> **Matthew 6:26 · ESV**
