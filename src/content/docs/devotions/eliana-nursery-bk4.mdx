*For Eliana — "My God has answered"*  
*held beneath the open sky,*  
*loved by the One who set it high,*  
*named before the clouds rolled by.*  


*Genesis 1:6–8 · ESV*

---

## Page 1 — God Spoke Again
🌬️

He spoke on the first day,  
and light was born —  
then God spoke again  
on the second morn,  
and something new  
began to form.

> *"And God said..."*
> **Genesis 1:6a (ESV)**

> *God only has to speak, and worlds obey — one word from Him and something new begins. That same voice, Eliana, knows your name and speaks it tenderly every single day.*

---

## Page 2 — Let There Be Space
🌊

*Let there be space,*  
*let there be room,*  
between the waters —  
a wide, wide womb  
where sky and sea  
could find their place,  
a vault of wonder,  
a holy space.

> *"Let there be an expanse in the midst of the waters, and let it separate the waters from the waters."*
> **Genesis 1:6b (ESV)**

> *God made room — wide, open, breathable room. He is a God who makes space for things to grow. He has made room for you too, little one, in His great and loving heart.*

---

## Page 3 — Waters Above, Waters Below
💧

Above the sky  
the waters wait,  
below the sky  
the waters rest —  
God held them both  
and called it great,  
He ordered all  
from east to west.

> *"And God made the expanse and separated the waters that were under the expanse from the waters that were above the expanse."*
> **Genesis 1:7a (ESV)**

> *What God separates, He holds. The waters above, the waters below — nothing spills, nothing falls, nothing is lost. He holds every part of His world, and He holds every part of you.*

---

## Page 4 — And It Was So
✅

He said it,  
and it was so —  
no arguing,  
no overflow,  
the waters went  
where God said go,  
and all creation  
learned to know:  
what God commands,  
His world below.

> *"And it was so."*
> **Genesis 1:7b (ESV)**

> *Four words — and it was so. God speaks and the whole world listens. His word never fails, never falls short, never comes back empty. What He says for you, little one, will surely come to pass.*

---

## Page 5 — He Named It Heaven
☁️

He called that wide  
and open space  
the Heaven — His  
most holy place —  
where clouds would float  
and birds would soar  
and children look up  
and ask for more.

> *"And God called the expanse Heaven."*
> **Genesis 1:8a (ESV)**

> *God named the sky Heaven. Every time you look up, you are looking toward the name God gave — and the God who named it is looking back down at you with love.*

---

## Page 6 — The Second Day
🌅

The evening came  
and hushed the light,  
the morning broke  
through veils of night —  
the second day  
was finished, good,  
held in the hands  
of God who could.  

> *"And there was evening and there was morning, the second day."*
> **Genesis 1:8b (ESV)**

> *God finished what He started — evening, then morning, the second day complete. He always finishes what He begins. And, Eliana, He is not finished with you yet.*

---

## ✦ Closing

> *"He stretches out the north over the void*  
> *and hangs the earth on nothing.*  
> *He binds up the waters in his thick clouds,*  
> *and the cloud is not split open under them."*  
> **Job 26:7–8 · ESV**
