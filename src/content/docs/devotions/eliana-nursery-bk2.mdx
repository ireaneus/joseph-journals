# Before the Morning
### A Nursery Book of Chosen Love

*For Eliana,*  
*chosen before the stars were hung,*  
*loved before the world was young,*  
*held before the morning sun.*

---

## Page 1 — Before a Single Day
🌌

Before God created  
a day or a night,  
or the darkness and light —  
He knew you, dear one,  
and called you His delight.

> Before God made anything — before light, before stars, before your very first breath — He already knew your name.

---

## Page 2 — He Had Something to Show
✨

Before the first morning,  
before the first song,  
before even silence  
had lasted too long—  
God whispered, "I want her  
to know where she belongs.

> God didn't make the world by accident. He made it to show something — His love, His kindness, His wonderful self.

---

## Page 3 — His Glory, So Bright
🌅

His glory is gentler  
than you might suppose —  
it shines in the morning,  
it blooms like a rose,  
it's written in you  
from your head to your toes.

> Everything beautiful — the light, the flowers, your little face — is God showing the world how good He is.

---

## Page 4 — The Potter's Hands
🏺

He held you so carefully,  
shaped you with care,  
the curve of your fingers,  
the curl of your hair —  
a Potter so loving,
beyond all compare

> God formed you with His own hands, Eliana — and called you His own before you drew your first breath.

---

## Page 5 — Mercy to Hold
🕊️

His mercy is deeper  
than wide rolling seas,  
more steady and soft  
than the whispering trees —  
He calls her His own  
she rests in His peace.

> God's mercy never runs out, Eliana. He gathered you close before you even knew His name — and He will never let you go.

---

## Page 6 — His Eyes Are Upon You
💛

He sees every tear,  
every laugh, every smile,  
He walks beside you  
each tiny small mile —  
and never, no never,  
will He leave you, dear child.

> God sees every single moment of your life, Eliana — every laugh, every tear, every tiny step. He has never once looked away from you.

---

## ✦ Closing

*Before the morning,*  
*before the light,*  
*God set His love*  
*on you that night.*

*And the God who made all things*  
*is holding you tonight.*
