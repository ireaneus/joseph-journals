# In the Beginning
### A Nursery Book of Creation
*Genesis 1:1–3 · ESV*

---

For Eliana — "My God has answered"  
little one so dear,  
held in the hands  
of the God who made the light  
and lovingly brought you here.

---

## Page 1 — Before It All
🌌

Before the world,  
before the sky,  
before a single breath or sigh,  
God was always there on high.

> Before anything existed — before light, before stars, before you — God was.

---

## Page 2 — God Made Everything
🌍

God made the heavens,  
God made the earth,  
by His word alone  
He gave all things birth.

> *"In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth."* — **Genesis 1:1 (ESV)**


---

## Page 3 — Dark and Still
🌑

The earth was dark,  
and still, and deep,  
like a quiet world  
still fast asleep.

> *"The earth was without form and void, and darkness was over the face of the deep."* — **Genesis 1:2a (ESV)**

---

## Page 4 — The Spirit Near
🕊️

God's Spirit moved  
so soft, so near,  
over the waters,  
*"I am here, I am here."*

> *"And the Spirit of God was hovering over the face of the waters."* — **Genesis 1:2b (ESV)**

---

## Page 5 — Let There Be Light!
☀️

Then God said softly,  
then God said bright—  
*"Let there be*  
*light, light, LIGHT!"*  

> *"And God said, 'Let there be light,' and there was light."* — **Genesis 1:3a (ESV)**

---

## ✦ Closing

> *"For God, who said, 'Let light shine out of darkness,' has shone in our hearts to give the light of the knowledge of the glory of God in the face of Jesus Christ."* - **2 Corinthians 4:6 · ESV**

*The same God who spoke light into the world loves **you**, little one,  Eliana.*
