*For Eliana — "My God has answered"*  
*you who are held in the light He named,*  
*loved in the order His goodness framed,*  
*known before the morning came.*

---

## Page 1 — God Saw the Light
✨

God saw the light —  
He looked, and smiled,  
the way a father  
looks upon his child.  
*It is good,*  
*it is good,*  
*it is good.*

> *"And God saw that the light was good."*  
> **Genesis 1:4a (ESV)**

> *When God looked at the light, He called it good — just as He looks at you and always would. You were made by that same glad, loving eye — the God who named the light has named you His.*

---

## Page 2 — He Divided the Dark
🌓

He took the light  
and set it here,  
He held the darkness  
over there, my dear —  
so both would know  
their place to stay,  
so night would be night  
and day would be day.

> *"And God separated the light from the darkness."*  
> **Genesis 1:4b (ESV)**

> *God did not fear the dark — He held it. He is Lord of all the night, and yet He chose the light for you. Even in the dark, He knows right where you are.*

---

## Page 3 — He Named Them Both
🏷️

He called the light  
by its name: *Day.*  
He called the dark  
by its name: *Night.*  
He is the God  
who names what's right.

> *"God called the light Day, and the darkness he called Night."*  
> **Genesis 1:5a (ESV)**

> *To name a thing is to know it, to hold it, to love it. God named the day. He named the night. And, Eliana, He has named you too — by name, and loved, and held in His sight.*

---

## Page 4 — Evening Came First
🌙

The evening came  
and wrapped the sky,  
the stars crept in  
to say goodbye —  
then slowly, slowly  
morning grew,  
and the whole wide world  
was clean and new.

> *"And there was evening and there was morning, the first day."*  
> **Genesis 1:5b (ESV)**

> *God counts His days from evening on — the dark comes first, and then the dawn. So when your nights feel long, my dear, the morning's coming. God is here.*

---

## Page 5 — One Good Day
🌅

One day was done —  
just one, and yet  
all heaven knew  
what God had set:  
a world worth making,  
a child worth keeping,  
a light worth naming,  
a love worth seeking.

> *The evening and the morning made the first day whole —*  
> *one day of goodness spoken from His loving soul.*  
> *Each day He makes is ordered, held, and right,*  
> *and every day you breathe is filled with His good light.*  

> *God made one day, and it was good. He makes your days, and they are good too — every morning that you wake is a gift from the God who separated the light just for you.*

---

## ✦ Closing

> *"The LORD is my light and my salvation — whom shall I fear?*  
> *The LORD is the stronghold of my life — of whom shall I be afraid?"*  
> **Psalm 27:1 · ESV**
