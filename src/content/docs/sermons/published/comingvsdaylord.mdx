## Wedding Pattern (Jewish Context)

- Bridegroom comes for the bride  
- Returns to the Father’s house  
- Wedding period (typologically seven days)  
- Returns with the bride  

---

## Feast Timeline (Prophetic Picture)

- Feast of Trumpets — Gathering  
- Day of Atonement (Yom Kippur) — Judgment / Tribulation  
- Feast of Tabernacles — Kingdom dwelling  

---

## Pretribulation Argument Summary

- **Coming of the Lord** → For His church (comfort, gathering)  
- **Day of the Lord** → Judgment upon the world  

---

## Revelation 4 Observations

- 24 Elders → Representation of redeemed people  
- White Garments → Imputed righteousness  
- Crowns → Rewards  
- Seven Spirits → Fullness of the Holy Spirit (*Isaiah 11:2*)  

---

## Coming vs. Day of the Lord (Summary)

| Coming of the Lord | Day of the Lord |
| ------------------ | --------------- |
| For believers | Judgment on unbelievers |
| Comfort | Destruction |
| Imminent | Preceded by signs |
| Gathering | Wrath |
