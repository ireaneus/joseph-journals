# In the Beginning
### A Nursery Book of Creation
*Genesis 1:1–3 · ESV*

---

*For my Eliana*
*whose every breath is held*
*in the hands of the God*
*who made the light.*

---

## Page 1 — Before It All
🌌

Before the world,
before the sky,
before the birds
could ever fly—

*Genesis 1:1a*

> Once there was nothing at all — no sun, no moon, no stars, no you. But God was there.

---

## Page 2 — God Made Everything
🌍

God made the heavens,
God made the earth,
everything good
was given its birth.

*"In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth." — Genesis 1:1 (ESV)*

> God made everything from nothing, just by His power and His word. That is how great He is!

---

## Page 3 — Dark and Still
🌑

The earth was dark,
and still, and deep,
like a quiet world
still fast asleep.

*"The earth was without form and void, and darkness was over the face of the deep." — Genesis 1:2a (ESV)*

> Empty and dark — but God was not worried. He had a wonderful plan!

---

## Page 4 — The Spirit Near
🕊️

God's Spirit moved
so soft, so near,
over the waters,
*"I am here, I am here."*

*"And the Spirit of God was hovering over the face of the waters." — Genesis 1:2b (ESV)*

> Like a mama bird over her nest, God's Spirit was gently watching over everything He made.

---

## Page 5 — Let There Be Light!
☀️

Then God said softly,
then God said bright—
*"Let there be*
*light, light, LIGHT!"*

*"And God said, 'Let there be light,' and there was light." — Genesis 1:3 (ESV)*

> God only had to speak — and light burst into the world! His word is that powerful.

---

## Page 6 — And There Was Light
🌅

And there was light!
So warm, so bright,
God filled the world
with golden light.

*Genesis 1:3b*

> Light filled everything — and God saw that it was good. His gifts are always good.

---

## ✦ Closing

*The same God who spoke light into the world loves **you**, little one.*

---

*"For God, who said, 'Let light shine out of darkness,' has shone in our hearts to give the light of the knowledge of the glory of God in the face of Jesus Christ."*

**2 Corinthians 4:6 · ESV**
