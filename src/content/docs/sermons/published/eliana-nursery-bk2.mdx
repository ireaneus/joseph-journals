

# Before the Morning
### A Nursery Book of Chosen Love
*Ephesians 1 · Romans 8 · Romans 9 · ESV*

---

*For Eliana,*  
*chosen before the stars were hung,*  
*loved before the world was young,*  
*held before the morning came.*

---

## Page 1 — Before a Single Day
🌌

Before God created  
a day or a night,  
or darkness and light—  
He chose you, little one,  
to hold His gentle light.

> Before God made anything — before light, before stars, before your very first breath — He already knew your name.

---

## Page 2 — He Had Something to Show
✨

Before the first morning,  
before the first song,  
before even silence  
had lasted too long—  
God whispered, *"I want them  
to know where they belong."*

> God didn't make the world by accident. He made it to show something — His love, His kindness, His wonderful self.

---

## Page 3 — His Glory, So Bright
🌅

His glory is gentler  
than you might suppose —  
it shines in the morning,  
it blooms like a rose,  
it's written in you  
from your head to your toes.

> Everything beautiful — the light, the flowers, your little face — is God showing the world how good He is.

---

## Page 4 — The Potter's Hands
🏺

He held you so carefully,  
shaped you with care,  
the curve of your fingers,  
the curl of your hair —  
a Potter so gentle  
with all that He bears.

> God made you on purpose, with His own hands and His own heart. There is nothing about you He didn't mean.

---

## Page 5 — Made to Carry Mercy
🕊️

He made you to carry  
something so rare —  
His mercy, His kindness,  
His gentlest care.  
A vessel of love  
for the whole world to share.

> God made some people to show just how merciful He is — to hold His love and let it spill out into the world.

---

## Page 6 — He Knew You First
🌿

Before your first laugh,  
before your first cry,  
before your first breath  
under God's open sky —  
He knew you completely  
and knew the reason why.

> God knew you before you were born. He looked at you and loved you — and He had a beautiful plan just for you.

---

## Page 7 — He Works in Everything
🌱

When days feel too heavy  
and hard to hold,  
when the night feels too dark  
and the world feels too cold —  
He's weaving your story  
in silver and gold.

> Even hard days are in His hands. God takes every single thing and works it into something good, because He loves you so much.

---

## Page 8 — Nothing Can Reach You
💛

Not the depth of the ocean,  
not the height of the sky,  
not a shadow, not sorrow,  
not a whispered goodbye —  
nothing can reach you  
where His love holds you high.

> Nothing — not ever, not anything — can take you out of God's love. You are held, little one. You are His.

---

## ✦ Closing

*The God who planned you before the morning*  
*is the same God who holds you right now.*  
*He is near. He is kind. He is yours.*

---

*And the God who made all things*  
*is holding you tonight.*
